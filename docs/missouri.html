Missouri - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Missouri, nonprofits received a total of $4.0B in government grants. Nonprofits in St. Louis city, St. Louis, and Jackson counties and 1st, 5th, and 7th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Bollinger, Laclede, and Mississippi counties and 8th, 4th, and 6th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Andrew, Audrain, and Barton counties and 8th, 6th, and 4th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
