Wyoming - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Wyoming, nonprofits received a total of $261.6M in government grants. Nonprofits in Sheridan, Laramie, and Natrona counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Big Horn, Platte, and Washakie counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Big Horn, Converse, and Johnson counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
