Wyoming - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Wyoming, nonprofits received a total of $261,596,078 in government grants. Nonprofits in Sheridan County, Laramie County, and Natrona County and all congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Albany County, Big Horn County, and Campbell County and all congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Albany County, Big Horn County, and Campbell County and all congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
