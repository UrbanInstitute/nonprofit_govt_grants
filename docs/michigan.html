Michigan - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Michigan, nonprofits received a total of $9.2B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, Michigan, and Wayne counties and received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Oscoda, Lake, and Dickinson counties and 10, 13th, and 8th congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Alger, Arenac, and Cheboygan counties and 10, 8th, and 7th congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
