North Dakota - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In North Dakota, nonprofits received a total of $455.7M in government grants. Nonprofits in Burleigh, Cass, and Ward counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Sioux, Bottineau, and Rolette counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Bottineau, Divide, and Emmons counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
