West Virginia - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In West Virginia, nonprofits received a total of $1.1B in government grants. Nonprofits in Kanawha, Cabell, and Monongalia counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Gilmer, McDowell, and Doddridge counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Boone, Calhoun, and Doddridge counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
