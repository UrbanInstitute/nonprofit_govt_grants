West Virginia - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In West Virginia, nonprofits received a total of $1.1B in government grants. Nonprofits in Kanawha County, Cabell County, and Monongalia County received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Braxton County, Pleasants County, and Wayne County would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Boone County, Calhoun County, and Doddridge County have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
