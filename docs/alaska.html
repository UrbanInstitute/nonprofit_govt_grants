Alaska - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Alaska, nonprofits received a total of $848.3M in government grants. Nonprofits in Anchorage Municipality, Matanuska-Susitna Borough, and Juneau City and Borough received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Aleutians East Borough, Aleutians West Census Area, and Anchorage Municipality would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Aleutians East Borough, Aleutians West Census Area, and Anchorage Municipality have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
