Alaska - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Alaska, nonprofits received a total of $848.3M in government grants. Nonprofits in Anchorage Municipality, Matanuska-Susitna Borough, and Juneau City and Borough received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Hoonah-Angoon Census Area, Aleutians East Borough, and Yakutat City and Borough would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Aleutians West Census Area, Bristol Bay Borough, and Copper River Census Area have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
