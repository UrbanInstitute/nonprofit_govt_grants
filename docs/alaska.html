Alaska - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Alaska, nonprofits received a total of $848.3M in government grants. Nonprofits in Anchorage Municipality, Matanuska-Susitna Borough, and Juneau City and Borough counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Kusilvak Census Area, Bethel Census Area, and Yukon-Koyukuk Census Area counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Aleutians West Census Area, Bristol Bay Borough, and Copper River Census Area counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
