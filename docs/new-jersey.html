New Jersey - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In New Jersey, nonprofits received a total of $5.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in Essex County, Mercer County, and Middlesex County and Districts 10, 6, and 12 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Atlantic County, Bergen County, and Burlington County and Districts 1, 10, and 11 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Atlantic County, Bergen County, and Burlington County and Districts 1, 10, and 11 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
