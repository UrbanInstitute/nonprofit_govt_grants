New Jersey - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In New Jersey, nonprofits received a total of $5.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in Essex County, Mercer County, and Middlesex County and Districts 10, 6, and 12 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Burlington County, Cape May County, and Sussex County and Districts 11, 4, and 5 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Salem County, Hudson County, and Middlesex County and Districts 8, 10, and 6 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
