New Jersey - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In New Jersey, nonprofits received a total of $5.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in Essex, Mercer, and Middlesex counties and 10, 6th, and 12th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Salem, Cumberland, and Hudson counties and 10, 8th, and 1st Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Salem, Hudson, and Middlesex counties and 8th, 10, and 6th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
