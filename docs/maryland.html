Maryland - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Maryland, nonprofits received a total of $6.1B in government grants. Nonprofits in Baltimore city, Montgomery, and Anne Arundel counties and 2nd, 8th, and 7th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Somerset, Worcester, and Garrett counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants.
