Maryland - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Maryland, nonprofits received a total of $6.1B in government grants. Nonprofits in Baltimore city, Montgomery County, and Anne Arundel County and Districts 2, 8, and 7 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Allegany County, Anne Arundel County, and Baltimore County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Allegany County, Anne Arundel County, and Baltimore County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
