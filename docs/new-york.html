New York - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In New York, nonprofits received a total of $29.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in New York County, Kings County, and Albany County and Districts 12, 10, and 20 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Hamilton County, Seneca County, and Ulster County and Districts 12, 17, and 1 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Allegany County, Chenango County, and Bronx County and Districts 5, 14, and 15 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
