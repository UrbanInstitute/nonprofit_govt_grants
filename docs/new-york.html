New York - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In New York, nonprofits received a total of $29.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in New York, Kings, and Albany counties and 12th, 10, and 20 Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Wyoming, Schoharie, and Greene counties and 5th, 8th, and 2nd Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Allegany, Chenango, and Bronx counties and 14th, 5th, and 15th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
