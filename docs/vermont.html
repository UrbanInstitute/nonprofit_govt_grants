Vermont - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Vermont, nonprofits received a total of $610.5M in government grants. Nonprofits in Chittenden County, Washington County, and Windham County received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Addison County, Bennington County, and Caledonia County would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Addison County, Bennington County, and Caledonia County have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
