Vermont - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Vermont, nonprofits received a total of $610.5M in government grants. Nonprofits in Chittenden County, Washington County, and Windham County received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Bennington County, Lamoille County, and Windsor County would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Washington County, Franklin County, and Rutland County have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
