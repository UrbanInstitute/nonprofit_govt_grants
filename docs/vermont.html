Vermont - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Vermont, nonprofits received a total of $610.5M in government grants. Nonprofits in Chittenden, Washington, and Windham counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Rutland, Franklin, and Orleans counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Washington, Franklin, and Rutland counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
