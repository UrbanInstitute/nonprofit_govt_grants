Florida - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Florida, nonprofits received a total of $12.4B in government grants. Nonprofits in Miami-Dade, Hillsborough, and Broward counties and 27th, 14th, and 2nd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Jackson, Hamilton, and Jefferson counties and 24th, 12th, and 2nd Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Calhoun, Dixie, and Gulf counties and 2nd, 24th, and 28th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
