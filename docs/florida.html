Florida - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Florida, nonprofits received a total of $12.4B in government grants. Nonprofits in Miami-Dade County, Hillsborough County, and Broward County and Districts 27, 14, and 2 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Franklin County, Gilchrist County, and Glades County and Districts 17, 16, and 11 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Calhoun County, Dixie County, and Gulf County and Districts 2, 24, and 28 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
