Oklahoma - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Oklahoma, nonprofits received a total of $1.6B in government grants. Nonprofits in Oklahoma, Tulsa, and Haskell counties and 5th, 1st, and 2nd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in McIntosh, Grady, and Woodward counties and 2nd, 3rd, and 4th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Adair, Alfalfa, and Atoka counties and 2nd, 4th, and 3rd Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
