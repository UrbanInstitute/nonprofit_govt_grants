Oklahoma - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Oklahoma, nonprofits received a total of $1.6B in government grants. Nonprofits in Oklahoma County, Tulsa County, and Haskell County and Districts 5, 1, and 2 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Blaine County, Ellis County, and Sequoyah County and Districts 1, 5, and 4 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Adair County, Alfalfa County, and Atoka County and Districts 2, 4, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
