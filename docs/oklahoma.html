Oklahoma - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Oklahoma, nonprofits received a total of $1,608,045,127 in government grants. Nonprofits in Oklahoma County, Tulsa County, and Haskell County and Districts 5, 1, and 2 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Adair County, Alfalfa County, and Atoka County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Adair County, Alfalfa County, and Atoka County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
