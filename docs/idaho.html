Idaho - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Idaho, nonprofits received a total of $643.1M in government grants. Nonprofits in Ada, Canyon, and Kootenai counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Lewis, Boise, and Franklin counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Adams, Boise, and Franklin counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
