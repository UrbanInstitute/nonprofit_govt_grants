Idaho - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Idaho, nonprofits received a total of $1.3B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, Idaho, and Ada counties and received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Lewis, Boise, and Franklin counties and would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Adams, Boise, and Franklin counties and have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
