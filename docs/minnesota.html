Minnesota - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Minnesota, nonprofits received a total of $4.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in Hennepin County, Ramsey County, and Olmsted County and Districts 5, 4, and 1 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Norman County, Becker County, and Wabasha County and Districts 7, 3, and 2 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Grant County, Lac qui Parle County, and Lake of the Woods County and Districts 4, 5, and 8 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
