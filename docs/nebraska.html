Nebraska - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Nebraska, nonprofits received a total of $986.9M in government grants. Nonprofits in Douglas County, Lancaster County, and Sarpy County and Districts 2, 1, and 3 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Arthur County, Webster County, and Saline County and Districts 2, 1, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Cass County, Cedar County, and Chase County and Districts 1, 3, and 2 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
