Nebraska - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Nebraska, nonprofits received a total of $986.9M in government grants. Nonprofits in Douglas, Lancaster, and Sarpy counties and 2st, 1th, and 3nd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Dixon, Sheridan, and Cherry counties and 3nd, 1th, and 2st Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Cass, Cedar, and Chase counties and 1th, 3nd, and 2st Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
