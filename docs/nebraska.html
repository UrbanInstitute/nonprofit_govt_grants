Nebraska - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Nebraska, nonprofits received a total of $986.9M in government grants. Nonprofits in Douglas, Lancaster, and Sarpy counties and 2nd, 1st, and 3rd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Dixon, Sheridan, and Cherry counties and 3rd, 1st, and 2nd Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Cass, Cedar, and Chase counties and 1st, 3rd, and 2nd Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
