Oregon - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Oregon, nonprofits received a total of $3.5B in government grants. Nonprofits in Multnomah County, Washington County, and Lane County and Districts 1, 3, and 2 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Baker County, Benton County, and Clackamas County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Baker County, Benton County, and Clackamas County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
