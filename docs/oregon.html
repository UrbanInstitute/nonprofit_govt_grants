Oregon - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Oregon, nonprofits received a total of $3.5B in government grants. Nonprofits in Multnomah, Washington, and Lane counties and 1st, 3rd, and 2nd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Gilliam, Grant, and Columbia counties and 2nd, 3rd, and 4th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Gilliam, Grant, and Lake counties and 3rd, 2nd, and 4th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
