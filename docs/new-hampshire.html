New Hampshire - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In New Hampshire, nonprofits received a total of $1.1B in government grants. Nonprofits in Hillsborough, Grafton, and Merrimack counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Coos, Merrimack, and Hillsborough counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Cheshire, Merrimack, and Coos counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
