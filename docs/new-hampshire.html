New Hampshire - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In New Hampshire, nonprofits received a total of $1.1B in government grants. Nonprofits in Hillsborough County, Grafton County, and Merrimack County received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Belknap County, Carroll County, and Cheshire County would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Belknap County, Carroll County, and Cheshire County have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
