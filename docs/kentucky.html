Kentucky - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Kentucky, nonprofits received a total of $2.4B in government grants. Nonprofits in Jefferson County, Fayette County, and Franklin County and Districts 3, 6, and 5 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Hart County, Metcalfe County, and Todd County and Districts 3, 2, and 6 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Adair County, Ballard County, and Bracken County and Districts 5, 1, and 6 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
