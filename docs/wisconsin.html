Wisconsin - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Wisconsin, nonprofits received a total of $6.3B in government grants. Nonprofits in Milwaukee, Dane, and Fond du Lac counties and 4rd, 2st, and 6th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Rusk, Menominee, and Douglas counties and 4rd, 7th, and 1th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Burnett, Lincoln, and Menominee counties and 4rd, 7th, and 2st Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
