Wisconsin - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Wisconsin, nonprofits received a total of $3.1B in government grants. Nonprofits in Milwaukee, Dane, and Fond du Lac counties and 4th, 2nd, and 6th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Rusk, Menominee, and Douglas counties and 4th, 7th, and 1st Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Burnett, Lincoln, and Menominee counties and 4th, 7th, and 2nd Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
