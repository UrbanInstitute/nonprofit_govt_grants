South Dakota - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In South Dakota, nonprofits received a total of $812,178,184 in government grants. Nonprofits in Minnehaha County, Pennington County, and Hughes County and all congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Beadle County, Bennett County, and Bon Homme County and all congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Beadle County, Bennett County, and Bon Homme County and all congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
