South Dakota - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In South Dakota, nonprofits received a total of $1.6B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, South Dakota, and Minnehaha counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Todd, Lyman, and Corson counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Brule, Corson, and Deuel counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
