South Dakota - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In South Dakota, nonprofits received a total of $812.2M in government grants. Nonprofits in Minnehaha County, Pennington County, and Hughes County received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Ziebach County, Gregory County, and Kingsbury County would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Brule County, Corson County, and Deuel County have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
