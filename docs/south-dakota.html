South Dakota - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In South Dakota, nonprofits received a total of $812.2M in government grants. Nonprofits in Minnehaha, Pennington, and Hughes counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Todd, Lyman, and Corson counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Brule, Corson, and Deuel counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
