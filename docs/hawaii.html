Hawaii - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Hawaii, nonprofits received a total of $1.1B in government grants. Nonprofits in Honolulu, Maui, and Hawaii counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Honolulu, Maui, and Hawaii counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Hawaii, Honolulu, and Maui counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
