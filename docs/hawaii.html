Hawaii - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Hawaii, nonprofits received a total of $2.2B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, Hawaii, and Honolulu counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Hawaii, Honolulu, and Hawaii counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
