Hawaii - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Hawaii, nonprofits received a total of $1.1B in government grants. Nonprofits in Honolulu County, Maui County, and Hawaii County received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Hawaii County, Honolulu County, and Maui County have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
