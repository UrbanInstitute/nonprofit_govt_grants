Hawaii - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Hawaii, nonprofits received a total of $1,081,113,515 in government grants. Nonprofits in Honolulu County, Maui County, and Hawaii County and Districts 1, and 2 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Hawaii County, Honolulu County, and Kauai County and Districts 1, and 2 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Hawaii County, Honolulu County, and Kauai County and Districts 1, and 2 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
