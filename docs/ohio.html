Ohio - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Ohio, nonprofits received a total of $8.0B in government grants. Nonprofits in Cuyahoga, Franklin, and Hamilton counties and 11th, 3rd, and 1st Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Marion, Vinton, and Madison counties and 3rd, 13th, and 4th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Harrison, Lawrence, and Morgan counties and 3rd, 15th, and 13th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
