Virginia - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Virginia, nonprofits received a total of $4.6B in government grants. Nonprofits in Arlington, Fairfax, and Alexandria city counties and 8th, 11th, and 9th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Amelia, Covington city, and New Kent counties and 9th, 7th, and 5th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Amelia, Bland, and Buena Vista city counties and 9th, 7th, and 3nd Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
