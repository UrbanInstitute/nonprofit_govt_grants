Utah - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Utah, nonprofits received a total of $3.3B in government grants. Nonprofits in Salt Lake, Utah, and Davis counties and 2st, 1th, and 3nd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Uintah, Box Elder, and Iron counties and 2st, 1th, and 4rd Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Box Elder, Carbon, and Iron counties and 2st, 3nd, and 1th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
