Utah - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Utah, nonprofits received a total of $1.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in Salt Lake, Utah, and Davis counties and 2nd, 1st, and 3rd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Uintah, Box Elder, and Iron counties and 2nd, 1st, and 4th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Box Elder, Carbon, and Iron counties and 2nd, 3rd, and 1st Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
