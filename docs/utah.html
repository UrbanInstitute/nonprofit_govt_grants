Utah - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Utah, nonprofits received a total of $1.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in Salt Lake County, Utah County, and Davis County and Districts 2, 1, and 3 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Wayne County, Sanpete County, and Garfield County and Districts 3, 4, and 1 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Box Elder County, Carbon County, and Iron County and Districts 2, 3, and 1 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
