Utah - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Utah, nonprofits received a total of $1,665,786,876 in government grants. Nonprofits in Salt Lake County, Utah County, and Davis County and Districts 2, 1, and 3 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Box Elder County, Cache County, and Carbon County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Box Elder County, Cache County, and Carbon County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
