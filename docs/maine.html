Maine - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Maine, nonprofits received a total of $2.3B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, Maine, and Cumberland counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Androscoggin, Aroostook, and Penobscot counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Aroostook, Androscoggin, and Penobscot counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
