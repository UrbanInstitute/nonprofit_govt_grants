Maine - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Maine, nonprofits received a total of $1.1B in government grants. Nonprofits in Cumberland County, Androscoggin County, and Hancock County received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Androscoggin County, Aroostook County, and Cumberland County would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Androscoggin County, Aroostook County, and Cumberland County have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
