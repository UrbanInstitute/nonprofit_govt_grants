Texas - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Texas, nonprofits received a total of $17.8B in government grants. Nonprofits in Bexar, Harris, and Travis counties and 21st, 9th, and 35th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Crosby, Cass, and San Saba counties and 28th, 16th, and 23rd Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Andrews, Archer, and Baylor counties and 28th, 20, and 23rd Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
