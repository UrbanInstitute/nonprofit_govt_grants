Rhode Island - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Rhode Island, nonprofits received a total of $1,009,094,930 in government grants. Nonprofits in Providence County, Kent County, and Newport County and Districts 1, and 2 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Bristol County, Kent County, and Newport County and Districts 1, and 2 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Bristol County, Kent County, and Newport County and Districts 1, and 2 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
