Rhode Island - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Rhode Island, nonprofits received a total of $1.0B in government grants. Nonprofits in Providence, Kent, and Newport counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Kent, Providence, and Washington counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Kent, Providence, and Washington counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
