Rhode Island - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Rhode Island, nonprofits received a total of $1.0B in government grants. Nonprofits in Providence County, Kent County, and Newport County received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Newport County, Bristol County, and Washington County would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Kent County, Providence County, and Washington County have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
