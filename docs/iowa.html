Iowa - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Iowa, nonprofits received a total of $2.5B in government grants. Nonprofits in Polk, Linn, and Scott counties and 3nd, 2st, and 4rd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Ringgold, Adams, and Cass counties and 3nd, 4rd, and 2st Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Buena Vista, Davis, and Delaware counties and 4rd, 3nd, and 2st Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
