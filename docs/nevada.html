Nevada - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Nevada, nonprofits received a total of $2.5B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, Nevada, and Clark counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Humboldt, Mineral, and Elko counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Humboldt, Mineral, and Nye counties and 4rd, 2st, and 1th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
