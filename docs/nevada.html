Nevada - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Nevada, nonprofits received a total of $1,252,051,703 in government grants. Nonprofits in Clark County, Washoe County, and Carson City and Districts 2, 1, and 4 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Carson City, Churchill County, and Clark County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Carson City, Churchill County, and Clark County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
