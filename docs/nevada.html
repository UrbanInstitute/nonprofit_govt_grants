Nevada - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Nevada, nonprofits received a total of $1.3B in government grants. Nonprofits in Clark, Washoe, and Carson City counties and 2nd, 1st, and 4th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Humboldt, Mineral, and Elko counties and 4th, 2nd, and 1st Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Humboldt, Mineral, and Nye counties and 4th, 2nd, and 1st Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
