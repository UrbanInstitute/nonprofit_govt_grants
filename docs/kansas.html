Kansas - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Kansas, nonprofits received a total of $1.0B in government grants. Nonprofits in Sedgwick County, Douglas County, and Johnson County and Districts 1, 2, and 4 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Anderson County, Barber County, and Cheyenne County and Districts 1, 2, and 4 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
