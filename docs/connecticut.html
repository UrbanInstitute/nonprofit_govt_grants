Connecticut - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Connecticut, nonprofits received a total of $3.8B in government grants. Nonprofits in South Central Connecticut Planning Region, Capitol Planning Region, and Greater Bridgeport Planning Region counties and 3rd, 4th, and 1st Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Southeastern Connecticut Planning Region, Naugatuck Valley Planning Region, and Northeastern Connecticut Planning Region counties and 1st, 3rd, and 2nd Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Northeastern Connecticut Planning Region, Naugatuck Valley Planning Region, and Greater Bridgeport Planning Region counties and 1st, 3rd, and 2nd Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
