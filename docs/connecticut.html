Connecticut - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Connecticut, nonprofits received a total of $3.8B in government grants. Nonprofits in South Central Connecticut Planning Region, Capitol Planning Region, and Greater Bridgeport Planning Region and Districts 3, 4, and 1 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Northwest Hills Planning Region, Western Connecticut Planning Region, and Lower Connecticut River Valley Planning Region and Districts 4, 5, and 2 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Northeastern Connecticut Planning Region, Naugatuck Valley Planning Region, and Greater Bridgeport Planning Region and Districts 1, 3, and 2 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
