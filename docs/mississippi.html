Mississippi - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Mississippi, nonprofits received a total of $855.1M in government grants. Nonprofits in Hinds County, Harrison County, and Washington County and Districts 2, 3, and 1 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Adams County, Alcorn County, and Amite County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Adams County, Alcorn County, and Amite County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
