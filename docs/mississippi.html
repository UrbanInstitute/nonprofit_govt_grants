Mississippi - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Mississippi, nonprofits received a total of $1.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, Mississippi, and Hinds counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Chickasaw, Jefferson, and Quitman counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Alcorn, Amite, and Attala counties and 4rd, 2st, and 1th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
