Mississippi - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Mississippi, nonprofits received a total of $855.1M in government grants. Nonprofits in Hinds, Harrison, and Washington counties and 2nd, 3rd, and 1st Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Chickasaw, Jefferson, and Quitman counties and 2nd, 4th, and 1st Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Alcorn, Amite, and Attala counties and 4th, 2nd, and 1st Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
