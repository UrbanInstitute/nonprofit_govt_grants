New Mexico - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In New Mexico, nonprofits received a total of $1.9B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, New Mexico, and Bernalillo counties and received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Colfax, De Baca, and Guadalupe counties and have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
