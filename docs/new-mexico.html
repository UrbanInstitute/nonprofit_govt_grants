New Mexico - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In New Mexico, nonprofits received a total of $943.7M in government grants. Nonprofits in Bernalillo, Santa Fe, and Doña Ana counties and 3rd, 1st, and 2nd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in San Juan, Sierra, and Valencia counties and 2nd, 3rd, and 1st Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Colfax, De Baca, and Guadalupe counties and 3rd, 1st, and 2nd Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
