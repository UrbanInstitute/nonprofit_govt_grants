California - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In California, nonprofits received a total of $84.3B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, California, and Los Angeles counties and received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Colusa, Sierra, and Siskiyou counties and 42st, 1th, and 6th congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Colusa, Lassen, and Sierra counties and 42st, 1th, and 9th congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
