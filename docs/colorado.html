Colorado - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Colorado, nonprofits received a total of $3.5B in government grants. Nonprofits in Denver, Jefferson, and El Paso counties and 1st, 7th, and 2nd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Gilpin, Crowley, and Saguache counties and 1st, 4th, and 8th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Conejos, Costilla, and Crowley counties and 1st, 4th, and 3rd Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
