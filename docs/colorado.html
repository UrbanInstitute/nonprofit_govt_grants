Colorado - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Colorado, nonprofits received a total of $3.5B in government grants. Nonprofits in Denver County, Jefferson County, and El Paso County and Districts 1, 7, and 2 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Cheyenne County, Hinsdale County, and Rio Blanco County and Districts 5, 7, and 6 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Conejos County, Costilla County, and Crowley County and Districts 1, 4, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
