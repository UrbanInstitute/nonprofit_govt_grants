North Carolina - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In North Carolina, nonprofits received a total of $7.2B in government grants. Nonprofits in New Hanover County, Durham County, and Wake County and Districts 7, 4, and 2 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Alamance County, Alexander County, and Alleghany County and Districts 1, 10, and 11 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Alamance County, Alexander County, and Alleghany County and Districts 1, 10, and 11 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
