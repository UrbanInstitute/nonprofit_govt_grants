Arizona - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Arizona, nonprofits received a total of $3.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in Maricopa, Pima, and Coconino counties and 3rd, 7th, and 5th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Graham, La Paz, and Mohave counties and 9th, 5th, and 7th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Graham, Mohave, and Pinal counties and 9th, 5th, and 7th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
