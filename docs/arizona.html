Arizona - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Arizona, nonprofits received a total of $3.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in Maricopa County, Pima County, and Coconino County and Districts 3, 7, and 5 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Gila County, Greenlee County, and Cochise County and Districts 1, 6, and 4 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Graham County, Mohave County, and Pinal County and Districts 9, 5, and 7 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
