Arizona - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Arizona, nonprofits received a total of $3.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in Maricopa County, Pima County, and Coconino County and Districts 3, 7, and 5 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Apache County, Cochise County, and Coconino County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Apache County, Cochise County, and Coconino County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
