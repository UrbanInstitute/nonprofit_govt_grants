Arizona - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Arizona, nonprofits received a total of $7.4B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, Arizona, and Maricopa counties and received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Graham, La Paz, and Mohave counties and 9th, 5th, and 7th congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Graham, Mohave, and Pinal counties and 9th, 5th, and 7th congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
