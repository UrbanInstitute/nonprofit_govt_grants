Georgia - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Georgia, nonprofits received a total of $5.2B in government grants. Nonprofits in Fulton, DeKalb, and Clarke counties and 5th, 2nd, and 10 Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Meriwether, Wheeler, and Wilkes counties and 14th, 12th, and 1st Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Appling, Bacon, and Banks counties and 1st, 2nd, and 12th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
