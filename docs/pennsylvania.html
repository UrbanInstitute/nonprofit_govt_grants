Pennsylvania - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Pennsylvania, nonprofits received a total of $26.4B in government grants. Nonprofits in Philadelphia, Allegheny, and Dauphin counties and 3nd, 12th, and 2st Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Cameron, Lawrence, and Pike counties and 16th, 7th, and 8th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Cameron, Fulton, and Pike counties and 16th, 7th, and 2st Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
