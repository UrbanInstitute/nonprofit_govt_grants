Massachusetts - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Massachusetts, nonprofits received a total of $20.3B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, Massachusetts, and Middlesex counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Franklin, United States, and Hampden counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Franklin, United States, and Bristol counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
