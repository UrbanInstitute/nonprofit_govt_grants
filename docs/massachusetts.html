Massachusetts - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Massachusetts, nonprofits received a total of $10.2B in government grants. Nonprofits in Middlesex, Suffolk, and Hampden counties and 7th, 5th, and 8th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Franklin, Hampden, and Barnstable counties and 1st, 2nd, and 7th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Franklin, Bristol, and Barnstable counties and 2nd, 7th, and 6th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
