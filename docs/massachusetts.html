Massachusetts - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Massachusetts, nonprofits received a total of $10.2B in government grants. Nonprofits in Middlesex County, Suffolk County, and Hampden County and Districts 7, 5, and 8 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Barnstable County, Berkshire County, and Bristol County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Barnstable County, Berkshire County, and Bristol County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
