Massachusetts - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Massachusetts, nonprofits received a total of $10.2B in government grants. Nonprofits in Middlesex County, Suffolk County, and Hampden County and Districts 7, 5, and 8 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Nantucket County, Dukes County, and Berkshire County and Districts 4, 5, and 9 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Franklin County, Bristol County, and Barnstable County and Districts 2, 7, and 6 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
