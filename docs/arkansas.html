Arkansas - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Arkansas, nonprofits received a total of $1.5B in government grants. Nonprofits in Pulaski County, Craighead County, and Sebastian County and Districts 2, 1, and 4 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Jackson County, Sharp County, and Hot Spring County and Districts 3, 4, and 2 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Arkansas County, Bradley County, and Calhoun County and Districts 1, 4, and 2 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
