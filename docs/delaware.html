Delaware - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Delaware, nonprofits received a total of $1.3B in government grants. Nonprofits in New Castle, Kent, and Sussex counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Kent, Sussex, and New Castle counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Kent, Sussex, and New Castle counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
