South Carolina - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In South Carolina, nonprofits received a total of $2.2B in government grants. Nonprofits in Richland County, Charleston County, and Greenville County and Districts 6, 4, and 7 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Abbeville County, Aiken County, and Allendale County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Abbeville County, Aiken County, and Allendale County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
