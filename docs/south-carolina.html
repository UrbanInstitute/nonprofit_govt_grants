South Carolina - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In South Carolina, nonprofits received a total of $2.2B in government grants. Nonprofits in Richland, Charleston, and Greenville counties and 6th, 4th, and 7th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Calhoun, Williamsburg, and Clarendon counties and 2nd, 5th, and 6th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Allendale, Barnwell, and Calhoun counties and 2nd, 6th, and 5th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
