South Carolina - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In South Carolina, nonprofits received a total of $2.2B in government grants. Nonprofits in Richland County, Charleston County, and Greenville County and Districts 6, 4, and 7 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Edgefield County, Laurens County, and Abbeville County and Districts 4, 1, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Allendale County, Barnwell County, and Calhoun County and Districts 2, 6, and 5 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
