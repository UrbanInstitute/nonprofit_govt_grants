Montana - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Montana, nonprofits received a total of $680.8M in government grants. Nonprofits in Missoula, Yellowstone, and Lewis and Clark counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Dawson, Pondera, and Hill counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Blaine, Dawson, and Liberty counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
