Washington - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Washington, nonprofits received a total of $12.9B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, Washington, and King counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Pend Oreille, Whitman, and Klickitat counties would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Lincoln, Wahkiakum, and Klickitat counties have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
