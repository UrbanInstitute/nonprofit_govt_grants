Washington - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Washington, nonprofits received a total of $6.5B in government grants. Nonprofits in King, Pierce, and Yakima counties and 7th, 9th, and 6th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Pend Oreille, Whitman, and Klickitat counties and 10, 5th, and 9th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Lincoln, Wahkiakum, and Klickitat counties and 10, 5th, and 9th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
