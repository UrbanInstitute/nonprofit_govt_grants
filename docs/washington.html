Washington - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Washington, nonprofits received a total of $6,452,428,730 in government grants. Nonprofits in King County, Pierce County, and Yakima County and Districts 7, 9, and 6 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Adams County, Asotin County, and Benton County and Districts 1, 10, and 2 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Adams County, Asotin County, and Benton County and Districts 1, 10, and 2 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
