Louisiana - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Louisiana, nonprofits received a total of $5.7B in government grants. Nonprofits in United States, Louisiana, and Orleans Parish counties received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Grant Parish, Morehouse Parish, and Concordia Parish counties and 5th, 3nd, and 4rd Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Allen Parish, Assumption Parish, and Caldwell Parish counties and 5th, 6th, and 3nd Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
