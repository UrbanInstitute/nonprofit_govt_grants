Louisiana - How Losing Government Grants Would Affect Nonprofit Finances

In Louisiana, nonprofits received a total of $2.9B in government grants. Nonprofits in Orleans Parish, East Baton Rouge Parish, and Jefferson Parish counties and 2nd, 1st, and 6th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Grant Parish, Morehouse Parish, and Concordia Parish counties and 5th, 3rd, and 4th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Allen Parish, Assumption Parish, and Caldwell Parish counties and 5th, 6th, and 3rd Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
