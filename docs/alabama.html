Alabama - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Alabama, nonprofits received a total of $3.9B in government grants. Nonprofits in Jefferson, Montgomery, and Mobile counties and 7th, 2st, and 3nd Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Pickens, Covington, and Sumter counties and 3nd, 4rd, and 7th Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Bullock, Butler, and Clay counties and 4rd, 1th, and 7th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
