Alabama - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Receiving Government Grants

In Alabama, nonprofits received a total of $1,944,864,228 in government grants. Nonprofits in Jefferson County, Montgomery County, and Mobile County and Districts 7, 2, and 3 received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Autauga County, Baldwin County, and Barbour County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Autauga County, Baldwin County, and Barbour County and Districts 1, 2, and 3 have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
