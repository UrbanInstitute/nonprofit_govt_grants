Illinois - Financial Risk of Nonprofits Losing Government Grants

In Illinois, nonprofits received a total of $10.0B in government grants. Nonprofits in Cook, McLean, and Sangamon counties and 7th, 1th, and 17th Congressional districts received the most government funding. Nonprofits in Pike, Putnam, and Pulaski counties and 12th, 1th, and 2st Congressional districts would have the biggest gap to fill if they lost their government grants. Nonprofits in Alexander, Bond, and Boone counties and 12th, 2st, and 1th Congressional districts have the highest share of nonprofits at risk of not covering their expenses if they lost their government grants.
